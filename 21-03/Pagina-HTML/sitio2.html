22 September 2010

\n

We are pleased to announce that a completely redesigned WinPcap website has been launched and is live as of this writing. The new site replaces the ten-year-old one \n that Loris Degioanni and I created when we were still at the university in Italy.

\n

Content remains the same: you will still find installers, source code, links, FAQs and much more. But there has been a general reorganization of information, along \n with a cleaning-up of spurious HTML code and hard-to-reach pages.

\n

We encourage everyone to visit the new website and provide feedback about the "lifting"!
